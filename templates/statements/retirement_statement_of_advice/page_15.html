Supplementary Information 4

Your Retirement Plan

Assumptions used for Your Retirement Plan:

These notes and assumptions should be read in conjunction with the projection.

It is important to remember the amounts projected are illustrations only and your actual results are not guaranteed in any way. Your actual future benefit amounts will vary depending on the actual contributions, investment returns, fees, and other external factors (such as tax and inflation).

Today's dollars

The results are shown in "today's dollars" so they are consistent with today's living standards. The inflation rate used varies based on our long term inflation forecast that we update monthly.

Administration fees

For the period up to the retirement age, an administration fee of $1.25 per week, plus an indirect administration cost of per annum of your account balance has been included in the projections.

Income account payments

The total pension payment in retirement (i.e. Income account payments from the super accounts plus the Government pension where relevant) has been indexed annually at 3.0% per annum. The minimum annual income payment percentages from your super account can be found at www.ato.gov.au/super. The Government pension rules are set by Parliament. The amount of the Government pension and other thresholds are updated regularly.

Investment return

The assumed annual investment return is customized according to your Risk Profile which determines your annual target return in excess of inflation. The higher your Risk Profile then the higher will be your excess return over inflation each year.

Contributions and taxes

All regular contributions have been indexed based on our long term inflation forecast. Contributions have been assumed to be paid regularly during each year. We optimize your retirement after- tax income based on your projected marginal tax rate.

No allowance has been made for the application of the Superannuation Guarantee (SG) maximum threshold, any tax payable on the projected super benefit at your retirement age and any tax payable or rebates on the projected pension payments in retirement.

Life expectancy

We calculate your life expectancy based on the standard ife tables published by the Social Security Administration and then adjust them based on your lifestyle choices.

Social Security

All estimated benefits are based solely on information provided by the Social Security Administration.
